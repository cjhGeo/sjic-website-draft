Competition Details

The competition will consist of two rounds, each designed to test different aspects of business strategy and innovation:

Preliminary Round: Teams will submit a 2-page written report and a 2-minute video pitch on their business idea.
Final Round: Shortlisted teams will present their proposals to a panel of judges at the final presentation event.
